**Programming style**

54% - rules or event-based  
_When Pacman eats all dots, he goes to the next level_

18% - global constraints
_Cannot go through a wall_

16% - declarations/other  
_There are 4 monsters._

12% - imperative  
_Display this string.  
Play this sound._
